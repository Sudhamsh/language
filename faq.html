I click the audio button but hear no sound. What should I do?

This is a common issue with text-to-speech in browsers. Follow these troubleshooting steps:

Quick Test: Open the Audio Diagnostic Tool to check if your browser supports audio and which voices are available.

For macOS Users:

  1. Check System Voices:
    • Open System Settings (or System Preferences)
    • Go to Accessibility ‚Üí Spoken Content
    • Click System Voice dropdown
    • Click "Manage Voices..."
    • Download voices for your languages:
      • Spanish: Download a Spanish voice (e.g., "M√≥nica", "Jorge")
      • French: Download a French voice (e.g., "Thomas", "Am√©lie")
      • Telugu: May use English voice as fallback
  2. Check Browser Volume: Ensure Chrome/Safari volume is not muted
  3. Try Different Browser: Chrome usually has the best voice support
  4. Restart Browser: Close and reopen after downloading voices

For Windows Users:

  1. Install Language Packs:
    • Open Settings (Win + I)
    • Go to Time & Language ‚Üí Language & Region
    • Click "Add a language"
    • Add languages you want:
      • Spanish (Espa√±a or M√©xico)
      • French (France)
    • Click the language ‚Üí "Options" ‚Üí Download Text-to-speech
  2. Enable Speech Settings:
    • Go to Settings ‚Üí Accessibility ‚Üí Narrator
    • Scroll to "Voice" section
    • Ensure voices are downloaded and available
  3. Try Microsoft Edge: Edge has better Windows voice integration than Chrome
  4. Restart Browser: Close and reopen after installing language packs

The voice sounds robotic or wrong. Can I improve it?

Voice quality depends on your operating system and browser:

  • Best Quality: Chrome on macOS/Windows with downloaded voices
  • Good Quality: Safari on macOS with Apple voices
  • Download Better Voices: Follow the steps above to download premium voices
  • Try Different Browser: Some browsers have better TTS engines

Browsers block auto-play audio until you interact with the page:

‚ú® Features & Usage

What are the keyboard shortcuts?
  • Space or Enter - Flip flashcard
  • Left Arrow - Previous card
  • Right Arrow - Next card
How does the quiz work?
  1. Click "üìù Take Quiz" from the flashcards page
  2. Select your language and level
  3. Choose number of questions (5, 10, 15, or 20)
  4. Answer multiple-choice questions
  5. Get instant feedback and see your final score
  6. Quiz tests both Native ‚Üí English and English ‚Üí Native
What does "Shuffle" do?

The üîÄ Shuffle button randomizes the order of flashcards. This helps with:

  • Preventing memorization of card order
  • Testing your actual knowledge
  • Making practice sessions more engaging
How do categories work?

Categories filter flashcards by topic:

  • Click "All" to see all 100 words in the level
  • Click a category (e.g., "Greetings", "Numbers") to see only those words
  • Great for focused learning on specific topics
  • Each level has 10-14 different categories
